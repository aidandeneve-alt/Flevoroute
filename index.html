Our Vision

Why Our Flevo Route is Unforgettable

Beyond the scenic views, the Flevo Route offers a journey through human ingenuity and breathtaking natural beauty.

This route has been meticulously designed to showcase the diverse wonders of Flevoland, the Netherlands' youngest province. You will experience the sheer awe of driving on land reclaimed from the sea, tracing the history of the massive reclamation project. The path connects monumental Land Art pieces, tranquil Nature Reserves like Bultpark, and historically rich locations such as Batavia Haven.

It's not just a drive; it's a dynamic, educational, and visually stunning exploration that links the best of Flevoland's natural, cultural, and engineering marvels. This carefully curated itinerary ensures every traveler leaves with a deep appreciation for the province's unique story.
